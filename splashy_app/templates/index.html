About Me

Hi! I'm Michelle Glauser, a lover of Python and homemade cookies. I learned to code at Hackbright Academy in 2012 and happily became a software engineer. I'm passionate about helping more women join and feel comfortable in tech. When I'm not doing techie things, I enjoy baking (and consuming) cookies, burying myself in a book, playing the piano, going on hikes, and watching dogs at a nearby park.
